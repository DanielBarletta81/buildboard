# BuildBoard Interactive Features Guide

## 🎯 Getting Started with Interactive BuildBoard

BuildBoard now includes powerful tracking features that help you manage your DIY project from planning to completion. All progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage.

## ✅ Completion Tracking

### How to Mark Items Complete
Every card (except Safety and Architecture) has a checkbox in the top-left corner:

- **Materials:** ☐ → Check when you've purchased the material
- **Cuts:** ☐ → Check when you've completed the cut
- **Tools:** ☐ → Check when you have the tool ready/acquired
- **Steps:** The numbered circle becomes a checkmark when you complete the step

### Visual Indicators
- **Green border:** Completed items have a green border
- **Strikethrough:** Completed item names are crossed out
- **Completion date:** Steps show when they were completed

## 📊 Progress Dashboard

At the top of every board view, you'll see the progress dashboard showing:

### Overall Progress
- **Progress bar:** Visual representation of completion
- **Percentage:** Shows X/Y cards completed (Z%)

### Board-by-Board Progress
- See completion for each board (Materials, Cuts, Tools, Steps, etc.)
- Quickly identify which areas need attention

### Budget Tracking
- **Estimated:** Your original budget from project planning
- **Actual:** Running total of what you've actually spent
- **Over Budget Warning:** Red text alerts you when you exceed your budget

## 🛒 Shopping List

### Adding Materials
1. Navigate to the **Materials** board
2. Click **"+ Add to List"** on any material card
3. The button turns gold when added: **"✓ In Shopping List"**

### Viewing Your Shopping List
1. Click the **🛒 Shopping List** button in the top-right header
2. Badge shows how many items are in your list
3. Materials are organized by supplier (Home Depot, Lowes, etc.)

### Shopping List Features
- **Total Cost:** See estimated total at bottom
- **Remove Items:** Click the red × to remove from list
- **Copy List:** Export entire list to clipboard
- **Shop Now:** Opens supplier website for quick purchasing
- **Product Links:** Click "View product →" for direct item links

### Typical Workflow
```
1. Add all materials you need to shopping list
2. Review grouped items by supplier
3. Copy list to clipboard
4. Shop online or in-store
5. As you purchase, mark materials as complete
6. Track actual costs as you buy
```

## 💰 Cost Tracking

### Tracking Actual Costs
1. Go to any **Material** card
2. Click **"track"** next to the estimated cost
3. Enter the actual cost you paid
4. Press Enter or click away to save
5. Your total budget updates automatically

### Viewing Budget Status
- Check the Progress Dashboard for budget comparison
- **Green text:** Under budget
- **Red text:** Over budget, shows by how much

### Tips
- Track costs as you purchase to maintain accurate budget
- Compare estimated vs. actual to improve future project planning
- Use for receipts tracking and expense reporting

## 🔍 Filtering & Search

### Status Filters
Located below the search bar:

- **All:** Show all cards (default)
- **Pending:** Show only incomplete items
- **Completed:** Show only finished items

### Search
- Type in search bar to filter cards by any text
- Searches across all card fields (name, description, specs, etc.)
- Works on currently active board

### Workflow Tips
```
Materials Board:
1. Filter to "Pending" to see what you still need to buy
2. Add pending items to shopping list
3. After shopping, mark as complete
4. Filter to "Completed" to verify purchases

Steps Board:
1. View "All" to see full sequence
2. Filter to "Pending" to focus on next steps
3. Mark steps complete as you finish them
4. Celebrate when all steps are done! 🎉
```

## 🎨 Visual Indicators

### Completion States
- **Unchecked:** White checkbox, normal border
- **Checked:** Green checkbox with checkmark, green border, strikethrough

### Shopping List
- **Not in list:** Gray button with "+ Add to List"
- **In list:** Gold button with "✓ In Shopping List"

### Board Tabs
- **Progress bars:** Thin green bar at bottom shows completion %
- **Counter:** Shows completed/total (e.g., 5/18)

### Step Numbers
- **Incomplete:** Gold circle with step number
- **Complete:** Green circle with checkmark

## 💾 Data Persistence

### Automatic Saving
- All progress saves automatically to browser local storage
- No account needed
- Works offline

### What's Saved
- ✅ Card completion status
- ✅ Completion dates
- ✅ Shopping list items
- ✅ Actual costs tracked
- ✅ Total spent
- ✅ Notes (future feature)

### Resetting Progress
1. Click **"Reset"** in Progress Dashboard
2. Confirms before deleting all progress
3. Cannot be undone - use carefully!

### Moving Between Devices
Currently: Progress is per-browser (not synced between devices)
Future: Cloud sync with user accounts (Phase 3)

## 🚀 Suggested Workflows

### Planning Phase
```
1. Review all boards to understand scope
2. Use search to find specific items
3. Note difficulty ratings and time estimates
```

### Shopping Phase
```
1. Go to Materials board
2. Filter to "Pending" 
3. Add all needed items to shopping list
4. Review shopping list, grouped by supplier
5. Copy list or use direct links to shop
6. Mark materials complete as you buy
7. Track actual costs
```

### Building Phase
```
1. Go to Steps board
2. View steps in sequence (All filter)
3. Check materials/tools needed for current step
4. Complete step, mark checkbox
5. Monitor progress dashboard
6. Reference Safety board as needed
```

### Completion
```
1. Review Completed filter on each board
2. Verify all materials marked as purchased
3. Confirm all steps finished
4. See 🎉 celebration when 100% complete
5. Export or document your project
```

## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Start with Materials:** Add everything to shopping list before first trip
2. **Batch Shopping:** Group items by supplier to minimize trips
3. **Track Costs Immediately:** Enter actual costs right after purchase
4. **Use Filters:** "Pending" view helps focus on what's left
5. **Reference Often:** Keep BuildBoard open during your build
6. **Check Progress Daily:** Watch that progress bar grow!
7. **Safety First:** Always review Safety board before starting new steps

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Where is my progress stored?**  
A: Browser local storage. It persists across page refreshes but is tied to your browser.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**  
A: Not yet - each browser has its own progress. Cloud sync coming in Phase 3.

**Q: What if I accidentally reset progress?**  
A: Reset is permanent and cannot be undone. Always confirm you meant to reset.

**Q: Can I edit the demo project?**  
A: Progress tracking works on the demo. Custom projects coming in Phase 2.

**Q: Does shopping list actually order items?**  
A: No - it's a planning tool. Use "Shop Now" or product links to purchase through suppliers.

**Q: What about multiple projects?**  
A: Currently one project per workspace. Multi-project support coming in Phase 2.
